ABOUT US

The extent of human impact on the Earth has reached dangerous levels today, which threatens long-term survival, progress and growth of the human civilization. We need to protect the planet from degradation by adopting principles of sustainable development, sustainable consumption and production, sustainably managing the natural resources and taking immediate action on climate change, so that we are able to support the needs of both present and future generations. Climate change is no longer a future threat but a stark current reality. We are already seeing the consequences of rising carbon dioxide concentrations, higher global temperatures and erratic weather pattern across the globe. Therefore, it is the need of the hour to “think globally and act locally.”

Since its inception, Tatva: Eco Club has been working towards environmental awareness at the very grass root level. And one way to equip the younger generation to deal with environmental concerns is through more effective environmental education and practicing them. We need to develop knowledge about the environment and establish a holistic approach towards living with the natural world, where lies the solution to all environmental problems faced by the human race today. Youth mobilization plays a significant role in sensitizing the society about daily do’s and don’ts in order to make a difference at an individual level. They can change their lifestyle and exhibit how it affects the environment. They can make their homes, schools, colleges more environment friendly by adopting eco-friendly practices, recycling different materials and saving the resources such as water, electricity etc. Here the role of youth is central, for it is in the rising generations that heightened awareness can most easily be achieved.

We also work with other stakeholders in order to achieve a common goal with different approaches. This truly helps in building consciousness for a developed and prosperous nation, where economic development and environmental growth function together.
